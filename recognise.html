RECOGNISE

Recognizing the signs of radicalization is crucial in preventing individuals from being drawn into extremist activities. It involves being aware of changes in behavior, language, and attitudes that may indicate someone is at risk. By understanding these signs, you can take proactive steps to address concerns and provide support to those who may be vulnerable.

SPOT THE WARNING SIGNS

PRETENCE

Pretending to be uninterested in family or friends, withdrawing from interactions.

REPETITION

Repeating opinions or views as though they are scripted, without engaging in open discussion.

EXHIBITING HATE

Exhibiting extreme unwillingness or inability to explain or defend personal views.

VOICING DISRESPECT

Voicing a sudden, disrespectful attitude towards others, particularly those with differing opinions..

EXPRESSIVE ANGER

Expressing disproportionate anger over minor issues or disagreements.

NOTABLY SECRETIVE/h5>

Notably becoming more secretive, especially when it comes to internet activities.

THOUGHTS INTERNALISED

Tending to avoid sharing thoughts, feelings, or experiences, increasing isolation.
